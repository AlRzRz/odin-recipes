Burgers

Description

WARNING: FLAVOR ALERT

This simple (yet amazing) food is one which is easy to create and delicious to eat.

The ingredients of this dish are fairly easy to find and somewhat simple. The enjoyment of a burger lies in its simplicity.

Ingredients:

  • A bun (Preferrably a brioche bun)
  • A preferred type of cheese (Cheddar and monterey jack go well with burgers)
  • A beef patty (Wagyu may go well)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Pickles
  • A chipotle mayo of some sort
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Cut the bun in half and set on your plate
  2. For 15 seconds, press both sides of your bun on a hot stove
  3. With a knife, spread the butter on both sides of the bun
  4. Set the cheese as a base
  5. Set the lettuce on top of the cheese
  6. Grill your patty to perfection
  7. Set the patty on the base you have created
  8. Pour some of your sauce on top of the juicy burger
  9. Set the rest of your vegetables on top of your creation
  10. Top off your bun
  11. Enjoy!
