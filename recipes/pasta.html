Description

WARNING: FLAVOR ALERT

This healthy and amazing food is a delicious intricacy that can be made in a variety of ways.

Today, we'll figure out how to create a basic tomato sauce pasta.

Disclaimer: This recipe is made to be used when you are short on time. You may focus more on the dish if you have a lot of time.

Ingredients:

  • Penne pasta
  • Premade/homemade tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella & Parmesan
  • Black pepper
  • Sea salt
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Prepare your pasta
  2. Once boiled and in the pan, mix with the tomato sauce
  3. Throughout the cooking process, drop in various amounts of cheese
  4. Drop in your cherry tomatoes by the end of the pan process
  5. Once done, place the pasta in the pan and drop a little more cheese
  6. Finish it off by seasoning it with sea salt, a little black pepper, and olive oil
  7. Enjoy!
