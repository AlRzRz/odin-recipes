Pizza

Description

WARNING: FLAVOR ALERT

Extremely easy to make and extremely delicious to eat.

A food that can range from casual to gourmet.

Ingredients:

  • Dough (or premade pizza bread)
  • Flour
  • Mozzarella
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Parmesan
  • Olive oil
  • Basil

Steps:

  1. Prepare your dough with the flour
  2. Once you have a base prepared, add olive oil (not too much)
  3. Add in your tomato sauce
  4. Sprinkle cheese and parmesan on top
  5. Add basil on top
  6. Bake
  7. Enjoy!
